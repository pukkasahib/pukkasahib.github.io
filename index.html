Writings Research Teaching Service and Outreach CV

PhD student working in computational protein biophysics at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Sarah Rauscher.

My work makes use of classical all-atom molecular dynamics simulations to address questions on protein allostery and functional dynamics. I am broadly interested in how correlated and high-fidelity dynamics emerge from the tertiary fold of proteins, and how we may better quantify protein allostery and state transitions on the single-molecule level.
