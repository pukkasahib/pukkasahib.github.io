About

Jane Doe

Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Biochemistry, University of Toronto. I use cryo-EM and X-ray crystallography to resolve structures of membrane proteins and understand how conformational dynamics drive function.

Previously I completed my PhD at the MRC Laboratory of Molecular Biology, Cambridge, where I worked on the structural basis of ion channel gating.

Cryo-EM X-ray crystallography Membrane proteins Ion channels Molecular dynamics Drug design
